Welcome to Societe Petroliere (SP)

PRODUCT-SERVICES

SP Ltd is the most reliable fuel supplier in Rwanda. We offer on-site refuelling through our 54 stations nationwide, bulk deliveries to our commercial or industrial customers and exportation throughout the region. You can trust that our high-quality products will arrive safely and on schedule. We deliver the following fuel products: Petrol, Diesel, Kerosene, A1 JET , Bitman, HFO, LFO.

As an environmentally conscious company, we are committed to making a positive impact on our planet by supporting the energy transition. We encourage our customers to reduce their carbon footprint by supplying LPG in cylinders or Bulk deliveries through our nationwide network of filling facilities. The LPG cylinders come in various sizes from 6 kg to 38 KG. We offer retail, wholesale, and bulk deliveries as well as LPG tank installations for our commercial clients. .

SP Ltd being the largest shareholder founded Africa Lubricant Manufacturing Company (ALMC) in 2013 to produce a range of Rymax lubricants in its modern oil blending plant located in Kigali’s primce economic zone. ALMC produces high-quality lubricants using virgin base oil sourced from Holland mixed with certified additives to meet the stringent specifications of the Rymax range of lubricants.

About Us

Since its inception, Societe Petroliere Limited (SP Ltd) has grown to be a market leader in the petroleum industry with the largest distribution channel of petroleum products, LPG and lubricants in Rwanda. The company boasts 63 petrol stations, 5 LPG cylinder filling facilities and a state-of-the-art fuel depot with a storage capacity of 65 million liters. SP Ltd has three business subsidiaries with SP Tanzania, SP Kenya and ALMC.
